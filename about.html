About the Club:

The Code Together Club was founded on the idea that together we can all tackle technical interviews comfortably. The club exist to provide a place where Berea College Students of all majors can come and gain the tools to pass technical interviews as well as tips on how you make sure your résumé makes you competative in your search for Internships.
